
Compliance Rating: 1-10 rating for how well the room stays the room budget and boost budget. More days that were less than room budget & /boosted budget on boost budget days equates to a higher score.

Warning Rating: 1-10 rating on how well the room refrains from warning looks at threshold warning safety shutoffs, and power cycles. Less warnings equates to a higher score.

Consumption Rating: 1-10 rating for how well the room keeps usage low. Lower usage equates to a higher score.

Load Rating
1 to 10 rating for how well the room limits time spent above 100 watts each day. Fewer hours above 100 watts per day result in a higher score.

Engagement Rating
1 to 10 rating for how consistently the user logs in each day to check the dashboard. More frequent daily check-ins result in a higher score. The criteria be if user checks twice with 12 hours of the day (not 12 times per day but count how many times user check dashboard within every hour perfect score is within 12 different hours user checked twice)

All the rating are generated from daily usage data and on going room totals. They will be save to a json file in /data and read from there and there will update every hour server side to recheck (server updates the json) front end loads from json so we have instant loading
